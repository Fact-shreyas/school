ABOUT US :

THE HERITAGE SCHOOL

The Heritage School,

was established in 2001, is a unique endeavour of the Kalyan Bharti Trust to recreate the ancient Gurukul tradition of India. Nestled in the lap of nature, the school provides an ideal atmosphere for learners to acquire and imbibe skills necessary for their physical, mental, social and intellectual development.
We believe that the world could be made a better place through education which focuses on concepts, ideas and issues that transcend disciplinary, cultural, national and geographical boundaries. To this effect, the school offers a variety of curricula ranging from the national ICSE and ISC to the international IGCSE and IBDP, with an aim to provide comprehensive education opportunities necessary for preparing conscientious, responsible and dynamic future global citizens. The Heritage School today is considered to be one of the leading international day-boarding schools not just in Kolkata, but in the whole country.
